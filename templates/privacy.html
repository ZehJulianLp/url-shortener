Privacy Policy

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our URL shortening service.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information from our users. The only data we collect is the original URL that you submit for shortening. This data is stored temporarily and is notassociated with any user accounts or personal identifiers.

How We Use Your Information

The original URLs you submit are used solely for the purpose of generating shortened links. We do not use this information for any other purposes, such as marketing or analytics.

Data Retention

We retain the original URLs for a limited period of time to ensure that the shortened links continue to function properly. After this period, the original URLs are deleted from our servers.

Third-Party Services

We do not share your information with any third-party services. Our URL shortening service is self-contained and does not rely on external analytics or tracking tools.

Your Rights

You have the right to request the deletion of any original URLs you have submitted. If you would like to exercise this right, please contact us via social media.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review it periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please feel free to contact us via social media.
